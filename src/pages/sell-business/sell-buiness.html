Want to join the nolk family?
Being self-starters ourselves, we know how significant the capital requirements associated with brand-building can be, from funding inventory to hiring talent. If you're finding it challenging to reach the next level, we might be able to lend a hand. We're keen to work with founders who want to see their business grow, but don't have the resources to make it happen.Using data, we are able to find asymmetries between brand value and economic performance in order to scale businesses to their full potential.
A distinctly modern
growth strategy
Optimizing success in today's world of saturated markets can be daunting. That's why we've made the process simple to understand.
Valuation
(5-10 days)*
This is where the conversation kicks off. We will begin an interview process and review key financial metrics to get a good grasp on the business health. If your brand is determined to be a good fit, we will make an offer and proceed to the next steps..
Due diligence
(15-45 days)*
If you accept the offer, nolk's team of experts begins the due diligence process, digging deeper into the financials, supply chain, marketing and inventory to get a deeper understanding of the business and then crafting a growth plan.
Agreement
(5-10 days)*
Legal documents will be finalized to cover all details of the transaction. Funds will be transferred to your account, and you can move on to your next venture.
*This can change depending on the type of business.
Integration
After the agreement is signed, your business is integrated into the nolk ecosystem.

After just 1 month, 90% of the business is integrated. During the next 11 months, you will only need to dedicate a couple of hours per month to address any specific questions that might arise during the final stages.
