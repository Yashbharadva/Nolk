Our commitments
Sustainability
We believe
Purpose is as important as profit and we're structured to prove it. From internal processes to supply chain partners and beyond, we have made it a priority to do good, and always try to do better. Our portfolio of brands is a reflection of this commitment, providing consumers with sustainable products they want, need, and care for.
Our commitments

We're part of 1% For The Planet and donate a portion of our revenue to help address social and environmental injustices.

Our Shopify stores offset the carbon emissions caused by shipping. Plus, we donate part of the profits from each order to support certified reforestation and forest preservation projects around the world.

We are in the process of obtaining the B Corp Certification.

In the next decade, we will undergo changes to become entirely carbon neutral. Nolk will be carbon neutral by 2035.

Sustainability is integral to all our brands.
Here are a few highlights.

Rachel uses biodegradable packaging for shipping. + Rachel partners with EcoCart to the offset carbon emissions of its Shopify shipments

Kana cookware is made from 40% reclaimed cast iron and packaged in uncoated paper and cardboard. Kana partners with EcoCart to offset the carbon emissions of its Shopify shipments.

Wolf and Grizzly uses only recycled fabric in all soft goods and partners with EcoCart to offset all carbon from shipping.

Loctote uses sustainable leathers and corn-based poly bags, while offering zero carbon shipping. Loctote and Freakmount partner with EcoCart to offset the carbon emissions of its Shopify shipments.

Opposite Wall posters are printed on FSC certified paper that is not only partially recycled but also manufactured locally. Additionally, the company donates 1% of annual proceeds to help support environmental initiatives through 1% for the Planet.

Ergonofis is a certified B Corp, 90% of purchasing is made in North America, and the company gives 1% of net profits to the David Suzuki Foundation.
